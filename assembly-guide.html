This page will guide you through assembling the Wearable Word Board step-by-step. Make sure you have all the printed parts and any needed accessories ready before starting.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Separate the bead segments from any support material carefully.
  2. Identify each bead group (A-M, N-Z, 0-9, etc.).
